Our paper on simulating enzyme catalysis with electrostatically embedded machine learning potentials has just been published in Chemical Science!

<!--more-->

Our paper demonstrates that enzyme catalysis can be captured by coupling efficient, reactive machine-learned potentials trained on gas phase data to the wider enzyme environment using electrostatic machine learning embedding.
You can check out the full article here: https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2026/sc/d6sc01156j
