Our PhD students Rubén Montagud and Luis Orta start their funded PhD period.

<!--more-->

We are pleased to announce that our PhD students Rubén Montagud and Luis Orta, who joined the group last October, have officially started their funded PhD periods.

Rubén has been awarded a GVA ACIF fellowship by the Generalitat Valenciana, while Luis receives a FPI fellowship granted by the Ministerio de Ciencia, Innovación y Universidades.
We congratulate them and wish them great success in this exciting stage of their doctoral research.
